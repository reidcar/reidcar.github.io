RSOcial 👩‍💻

Autumn '24 • INFO 360

RSOcial is a mobile app prototype designed to make UW's student organizations more discoverable and accessible. It reimagines the outdated HuskyLink platform by centralizing information, enabling direct communication, and creating a more intuitive, student-centered experience.

Problem Statement: How might we make RSOs more discoverable for UW students to join so students can better experience social and personal growth while at University of Washington?
