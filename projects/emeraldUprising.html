Emerald Uprising

Designed for INFO 498 - UX Design of Games (Spring ’25)

Game Pitch

What if you could save the planet – with just a Water Gun?
Think Wall-E’s representation of a polluted future, the whimsical plant-based combat of Plants vs. Zombies, and the strategic FPS intensity of Valorant.
Emerald Uprising is a co-op FPS where you and your squad become Eco Warriors, fighting enemies not with bullets, but with nature. Set in a world choking on environmental collapse, you’ll take on missions across diverse biomes – from polluted cities to oil-slicked oceans to forest wildfires. You’ll battle smog, fire, and trash monsters with an eco-powered arsenal. That fire monster destroying the once bright green forest? Use your Water Gun to extinguish its flames then regenerate the surrounding plant life. Throw your Seed Grenade to disable nearby enemies and see a burst of flourishing plants erupt from the earth, purifying the air and restoring terrain. Your weapons don’t just fight, they heal.
You’ll pick a struggling biome, fight waves of pollution-based monsters, and complete real-world inspired challenges to clean up the environment.
What sets us apart is this dynamic interplay between combat and environmental restoration. Seed Grenades don't only immobilize monsters but purify the air and create healing zones for your allies. Every shot fired creates life instead of death. Weapons aren’t just for destruction, but also for growth.
Flipping the FPS genre on its head, Emerald Uprising is a co-op combat game with a cause: it’s thrilling, strategic, and relentlessly hopeful.
